INTERSTELLAR

"Interstellar" is a 2014 sci-fi film directed by Christopher Nolan. It explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the survival of humanity as a group of astronauts travels through a wormhole in search of a new habitable planet. The film features stunning visuals, a complex narrative involving time dilation, and a memorable score by Hans Zimmer. Its portrayal of theoretical physics, particularly around black holes and relativity, has sparked much discussion and analysis.
